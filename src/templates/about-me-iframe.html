Always Creating Something!

Welcome to my corner of the internet! Hey there, I’m Tran Huy Thai, though online I often go by Tranpoline. I’ve always loved making things, from painting and pottery to tinkering with electronics and hardware, I get a thrill out of bringing ideas into reality.

Over the past few years, I’ve shifted much of that creative energy into the world of software development, exploring how machine learning and deep learning can unlock new possibilities. Many of my projects start as wild “what if” ideas, and I enjoy figuring out how to make them real through code. My journey in tech is deeply tied to my curiosity and imagination. I believe that if an idea can be thought of, there is always a way through persistence, creativity, and the right tools to make it happen.

This blog is my way of collecting projects, experiments, and reflections in one place where others can follow along, learn from my mistakes, or even get inspired to build something of their own. So stick around, I’d love to share my journey, ideas, and creations with you!
